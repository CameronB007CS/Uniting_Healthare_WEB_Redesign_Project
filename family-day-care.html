Home/Family Day Care
🏡 Home-Based Care

Small group care, in a real home environment

Uniting Family Day Care connects families with registered, in-home educators providing flexible, personalised early childhood education and care for children aged 0–12.

📞 1800 864 846
What's included

What we offer in Family Day Care

👶

Small Group Sizes

Family Day Care educators care for small groups (max 7 children) — far more individual attention than a large centre.

🕐

Flexible Hours

Many educators offer early mornings, evenings, weekends, or overnight care — flexible when standard centres can't help.

🏡

Home Environment

A warm, domestic setting that many children find more comfortable than a large centre — especially infants and toddlers.

📚

Qualified & Supported

All educators are qualified, registered, and supported by our coordination team — with regular monitoring and professional development.

🌈

Culturally Diverse

Choose an educator from a similar cultural background if important to your family — language, food, and customs can be maintained.

👨‍👩‍👧

Mixed Age Groups

Children of different ages learn from each other in a natural family-like group — good preparation for school and life.

How it works

Getting started

1

Contact our coordinator

Tell us about your child, your hours, and your location. We'll find compatible educators in your area.

2

Meet educators

We arrange meet-and-greet visits so you can meet potential educators and see their homes before committing.

3

Enrol your child

Complete enrolment paperwork. Your Child Care Subsidy is applied from the start date.

4

Care begins

A settling-in period is arranged. Our coordinator checks in regularly to make sure everything is going well.
